# Rev D Assembly Notes

- Treat Rev D as a bench prototype only.
- Populate power input protection and 5 V buck first.
- Do not insert TWN4 reader until the 5 V rail is verified under current limit.
- Keep lock output unloaded during first power-up.
- Verify RS-485 A/B labeling against the host-side adapter before connecting to a real OSDP bus.
- Use current-limited bench supply for all first tests.
