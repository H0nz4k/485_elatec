# Rev D Known Risks

- X2 mechanical alignment is based on documentation and must be physically checked against the real TWN4 reader.
- Connector mating height and standoff height are not finalized.
- 5.08 mm terminal blocks are probably too large for the final compact product.
- RPi gateway hardware is intentionally excluded from Rev D compact carrier.
- Autorouted buck power loop is not production-quality.
- Autorouted lock/relay power paths require current-width review.
- RS-485 polarity and OSDP firmware behavior require bench testing.
- BOM is not fully LCSC/JLCPCB sourced.
- Gerbers include extra layers and need filtering before upload.
